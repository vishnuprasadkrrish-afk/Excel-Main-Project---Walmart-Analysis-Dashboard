# Excel-Main-Project---Walmart-Analysis-Dashboard
Its about the Walmart Analysis Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Walmart Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel.The dashboard provides insights into Walmart's performance across states, metro areas, departments and stores.It offers a consolidated view of key business metrics such as market health, weekly sales,foreclosure ratios, size ranking by city and sales vs temperature.

Overview
The dashboard shows the top 10 states by average market health index, top 10 metro areas by profit, department-wise weekly sales, sales vs temperature, foreclosure ratio by city, size rank by city and year-wise sales by store.

Key Insights
States like TX and WA show strong market health indicators.
Metro areas such as New York and Miami lead in profitability.
Certain departments consistently outperform others in weekly sales.
Temperature has a noticeable correlation with sales fluctuations.
Some cities show higher foreclosure ratios,impacting store performance.
City size ranking helps identify potential markets for expansion or optimization.
